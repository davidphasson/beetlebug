• For Schools Coordinating with Other Schools

    If your school is not in O.C., S.D., L.A., Riverside, Imperial, San Bern. counties in California, then you must indicate all the schools you're coordinating with.
    (Note: outlying areas of L.A., R., Imp., SanB., counties may require 2 schools - we'll let you know.)

    Here's the minimum # schools breakdown for California:

    Southern Desert Areas: 2 schools
    Most of Central Valley: 4 schools
    Southern Coastal: 3 schools
    ALL Northern: 5 schools

    Dates will be scheduled once we have forms from ALL the schools you're coordinating with. It is not unusual for the minimum # of schools to be exceeded quickly and we end up booking an entire week in an area that might only have a minimum of 2 schools required, which is fine. Once schools start coordinating, other schools often want to get on board. All schools interested should submit and this Assembly Request Form, as we may be able to start working on filling a second week for your area - if it gets to that point.

    Indicate DATE, NAME OF SCHOOL, CONTACT PERSON that you are coordinating with. This helps us know who we should be keeping an eye out for. It does not mean that we will initiate contacting them - they need will be responsible for initiating the request. If you need to check back with us, we'll be able to tell you who got their forms in and who hasn't.

    EXAMPLE:
    Mon, March 4 - Lotsafun Elmentary - Ima Goodreader - Library - email@school.edu
    Tue, March 5 - Lakewood Elementary - Jenny SoAndSo - PTA - email@school.edu
    Wed, March 6 - Sunrise Elementary - Bill CoolGuy - Principal - email@school.edu
    Thur, March 7 - Railroad Elementary - Doris ILikeBooks - Library - email@school.edu
    Fri, March 8 - Surfsup Elementary - Mrs. SurferGal - Teacher - email@school.edu
